Then [God Will Restore] the high priest rose up with his brothers the priests and built the Sheep Gate. They consecrated it and set its doors in place, as far as the Tower of [A Hundred] and the Tower of [God Is Gracious / Gracious Gift of God].
Next to him [Men of the City of the Moon / Fragrance] built, and next to them [Mindful / One Who Remembers] son of [The Word / Eloquent] built.
The sons of [Thorny / Bramble] rebuilt the Fish Gate; they laid its beams and put its doors and bolts and bars in place.
Next to them [Excellences / Heights] son of [God Is Light / Flame of Jehovah], son of [The Thorn / Nimble], made repairs. Next to them [Friend / Allied] son of [Blessing / Kneeling], son of [God Delivers], made repairs. And next to them [Righteous / Just] son of [In the Affliction] made repairs.
Next to them [Jehovah Knows] son of [Passover / Limping] and [Friend / Allied] son of [In His Counsel] repaired the Old (or Jeshanah) Gate; they laid its beams and put its doors and bolts and bars in place.
Next to them [Repaired] son of [Imprisonment / Incense] and the inhabitants of [The Place of Palms / Fragrant].
Joiada son of [Peaceful] and [Friend / Allied] son of [In the Counsel / Foundation of God] repaired another section, as far as the house of [The High / Exalted].
(Skipping some group sections like the Tekoites [who are "Trumpet Sounders" or "Fixed Measure" in some etymologies, but often left as place-based], and continuing the pattern...)
[The King Will Establish / My King Is Jah] son of [The Captive / Hopeful] repaired another section, opposite the ascent to the armory at the Angle.
[The Blessed of Jah] zealously repaired another section, from the Angle to the door of the house of [God Has Kept / Eliashib / God Will Restore] the high priest.
After him [Consolation / Comforted of God] son of [The Exalted One] repaired another section...
And so on through goldsmiths [Ones Working with Gold], perfumers [Ones Making Ointment / Sweet Spices], merchants, priests, rulers of districts like [House of the Vineyard / Enclosure of the Vineyard], [Son of Rechab / Rider], [The Dung Gate / Refuse], the Fountain Gate, the Water Gate, etc.
